Release Notes on December 26, 2016 for v0.16.6

Dec 25, 2016

We are releasing sooner than we use to for this special day :christmas_tree::gift:. 17 contributors are making that last release of the year possible.

2016 has been an exceptional year for Material-UI.

  • We went from 40k to 180k downloads a month. :package:
  • We went from 12k to 22k stars. :star:

That wouldn't have been possible without this awesome community. Thank you!

But this's just the beginning, some exciting stuffs are coming with 2017 :sparkles:. You can preview a very early version of the next branch following that link.
